# Architecture Exterior Prompts

## A. Residential Exterior Concept

Prompt:

```text
realistic architectural exterior concept of [BUILDING_OR_STRUCTURE], practical construction, natural daylight, realistic materials, human-scale proportions, clean composition
```

## B. Small Garden Structure

Prompt:

```text
realistic outdoor structure concept for [STRUCTURE], garden setting, practical buildable proportions, natural wood or metal materials, weather protection, soft daylight
```

## C. Rain Protection Concept

Prompt:

```text
realistic architectural rain protection concept for [STRUCTURE_OR_ENTRY], wet ground, visible roof coverage, practical drainage direction, realistic materials, overcast daylight
```

## D. Sun Shading Concept

Prompt:

```text
realistic architectural sun shading concept for [STRUCTURE_OR_FACADE], strong daylight, shaded usable area, practical roof or louver design, realistic materials
```

## E. Material Variant Concept

Prompt:

```text
realistic architectural material variant for [STRUCTURE], showing [MATERIAL], practical buildable proportions, clean daylight, no text, no labels
```
